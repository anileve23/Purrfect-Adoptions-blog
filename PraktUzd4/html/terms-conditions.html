Terms and Conditions

Welcome to Purrfect Adoptions! We're thrilled that you're considering adopting a cat from us. Below are the terms and conditions that outline our adoption process and responsibilities:

Adoption Process

1. Interested adopters must fill out an adoption application form.

2. Once the application is received, our team will review it and contact you for further details.

3. Approved adopters will have the opportunity to meet and spend time with the available cats.

4. If a match is found, the adoption fee will be processed, and necessary paperwork completed.

Responsibilities

1. Adopters are responsible for providing a loving and safe environment for the adopted cat.

2. Regular veterinary care, including vaccinations and check-ups, should be maintained.

3. Adherence to local animal welfare laws and regulations is required.

Legal Disclaimers

1. Purrfect Adoptions holds no liability for any damage or injury caused by the adopted cat post-adoption.

2. We reserve the right to deny any adoption applications without providing explanations.

By proceeding with the adoption process, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to the terms and conditions set forth by Purrfect Adoptions.
